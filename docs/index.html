Welcome

Composition Book Media is a wide-ruled media company focused on producing innovative content through artistic partnerships and collaborations in the areas of film, television, publishing, media, and fine art.

This site is designed like a real composition notebook: a working object, a creative tool, a place where ideas are drafted, refined, and carried into the world. Each page holds a different part of the studio practice, from projects in development to production services and collaborative opportunities.

Open notebook. Start here.

Page 5

Working Method

Composition Book Media approaches each project like a notebook in active use: ideas are collected, drafted, sharpened, rearranged, and made legible through collaboration. The goal is not only to produce content, but to build forms that feel authored, precise, and alive.

We are interested in thoughtful partnerships across the entertainment industry and beyond, especially work that values artistic identity, durable craft, and imaginative structure.
